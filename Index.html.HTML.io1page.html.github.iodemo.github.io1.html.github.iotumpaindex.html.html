 Why not others, Only Tumpa D.? 

Tumpa D. is a top graphic designer in Kolkata with more than 7 years of designing expiriences. I am always ready to give instant services for our client. I can provide entire services from design to finish (Design, Prepress, Print and Finishing). You can go with my portfolio link to confirm.

 How many revisions do I get?  

Most of the graphic artists follow their particular revisions limit. That isn’t really the way to get you the best product, is it? I believe on job satisfaction only, may be it comes from our designer's term like within one revision or some time it comes after three or four revisions. Creation never measured by its numerical term, when it comes just happened with magic.

 How long does a project take?

On the basis of unique nature of every project, a project timeline can vary from weeks to months. However, before a project is started, clients are informed of what to expect based on all information provided to the designer from the client.

 How much do you charge?

There are three early estimates that are needed for a project--effort, duration, and cost. Of the three, effort hours must be estimated first. It is my principle to be transparent with our each and every client with all costs itemized prior to the start of any project. Any changes and adjustments are then made and agreed upon by both sides before moving towards. Thats why we do paper work first and send to you through only email and after confirmation of 50 % charges, we will proceed.
