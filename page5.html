Hello,
Meet Tumpa...

I've always introduced myself as an illustrator and a presentation designer, exploring innovative storytelling and bringing delight to presentations. As a designer my job is to communicate a client's story, brand and ideas. I’m also much more.

I’m a logo designer, a photographer, a motion designer, a teacher, and a lifetime student. I’m an optimist. I’m a big nature lover. I’m a Kolkata native with a passion for seeing all parts of the India. I worry I won’t be able to read all the books I want in this lifetime. But if I had to choose a different lifetime I’d pick the 90s/2k.

I am passionate about the world of design. I won’t get far in design if I don’t have a love of art or an interest in the world around you. Self doubt is a little part of my characteristics. As a result I continually assessing my decisions and striving to do better. It’s healthy to question and challenge myself.
